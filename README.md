# Nyan Cat: Flappy Flight!

## Overview

Welcome to Nyan Cat: Flappy Flight! A delightful twist on the classic Flappy Bird formula, featuring the iconic Nyan Cat soaring through a vibrant, meme-filled world. Navigate through the colorful cosmos, avoid obstacles, and collect points to achieve the highest score!

## Gameplay

### Objective

Guide Nyan Cat through the gaps between the pipes and avoid colliding with them. The game continues until Nyan Cat collides with a pipe or the ground.

### Player Controls

*   **Flap:** Tap the screen (or press the designated key on a keyboard) to make Nyan Cat flap its wings and ascend.
*   **Keep Flapping:** Continuous tapping will keep Nyan Cat airborne.

### Obstacles

*   **Pipes:** Green pipes, inspired by the original Flappy Bird, are strategically placed to challenge your flying skills. They appear at random heights, and you must navigate Nyan Cat through the gaps.

### Scoring

*   **Points:** You earn **1** point for successfully passing each set of pipes.
*   **High Score:** The game keeps track of your highest score. Try to beat your personal best!

### Visuals

*   **Nyan Cat:** The star of the show! A pixelated Nyan Cat, complete with a rainbow trail, takes center stage.
*   **Pipes:** The classic green pipes, now with a Nyan Cat twist!
*   **Background:** A vibrant, space-themed parallax background that moves at different speeds, creating an illusion of depth and adding to the game's charm.
